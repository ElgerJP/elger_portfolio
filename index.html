How I Usually Do Things

These are the steps that I normally follow whenever I have a project to make. It works for me and I think that it brings good results.

1

Analyse/Discuss The Project

I see throughout all the requirements and analyse what would be the best way to design it, I note all the ideas and go to the next step.

2

Brain Storming & Concept

I show my ideas to friends and colleagues, always trying to take the best of it and adapting it, if necessary.

3

Project Build

It is time to build the project now, I will use all available tools and all knowledge that I have acquired to bring the concept alive.

4

Interaction and Feedback

I show the final project to others and expect their feedbacks, if any change is required or seen needed, I go back to step two.
